We are there when patients
need us the most

Our solutions are customized to each brand team that we partner with, focusing on its most important priorities as they relate to engaging patients and their caregivers.

We focus on collecting and synthesizing all appropriate data from those interactions, enabling our partners to gain unique insights into the use of their medications in the real world.

We focus on...

  • …ensuring that patients have access to life-altering medications
  • …minimizing the bureaucratic and administrative burden for patients and physician offices
  • …ensuring that all eligible patients are informed of the treatments options available to them
  • …enabling a deep understanding of patient and physician preferences, behaviors & attitudes

We work closely with our clients to customize a patient engagement program that is uniquely tailored to the needs of the specific treatment

example 1

Treatment initiation

Patient receives a welcome call to introduce all the services available to them, reassure them that at this difficult time of disease diagnosis and treatment initiation they will get all the help they need, and ensure their needs are met.

Subsequently, our care team will take care of any and all details needed to ensure that the patient quickly receives their life-altering therapy: Ensuring all appropriate documentation is in pace, liaising with a specialty pharmacy as needed, managing co-pay assistance programs for eligible patients, liaising with insurance companies for benefits investigation and verification, working with physician offices and patients to manage any step edits or prior authorizations, coordinate free drug programs, provide any available financial assistance programs, etc.

example 2

Adherence

Our teams are trained to understand the underlying reasons behind treatment discontinuations, and then address those reasons with targeted interventions.

From routine adherence calls, to offering bridge drug as needed, understanding adverse events, recommending any available financial assistance programs, and empowering patients to overcome any emotional or other barriers to their treatment.

We focus on collecting and synthesizing all appropriate data from those interactions, enabling our partners to gain unique insights into the use of their medications in the real world.

We focus on...

  • …ensuring patients have access to life-altering medications
  • …minimizing the bureaucratic and administrative burden for patients and physician offices
  • …ensuring all eligible patients are informed of the treatments options available to them
  • …enabling a deep understanding of patient and physician preferences, behaviors, and attitudes

We work closely with our clients to customize a patient engagement program uniquely tailored to the needs of the specific treatment

example 1

Treatment initiation

Each patient receives a welcome call to introduce all the services available to them, reassure them that, at this difficult time of disease diagnosis and treatment initiation, they will get all the help they need and ensure their needs are met.

Subsequently, our care team will take care of any and all details needed to ensure the patient quickly receives their life-altering therapy: Ensuring all appropriate documentation is in place, liaising with a specialty pharmacy as needed, managing co-pay assistance programs for eligible patients, liaising with insurance companies for benefits investigation and verification, working with physician offices and patients to manage any step edits or prior authorizations, coordinating free drug programs, providing any available financial assistance programs, etc.

example 2

Adherence

Our teams are trained to understand the underlying reasons behind treatment discontinuations, and then address those reasons with targeted interventions:

From making routine adherence calls, to offering bridge drug as needed, understanding adverse events, recommending any available financial assistance programs, and empowering patients to overcome any emotional or other barriers to their treatment.

example 3

Data & Insights

When a patient interacts with their case manager, the issue at hand (e.g., dealing with all the paperwork and approvals needed to ensure their insurance company quickly reimburses the drug prescribed by their doctor) is often only half the battle.

As part of that interaction, a wealth of real-world and nuanced data about the patient and their disease is being exchanged: from details around doctor visits, to their broader care environment, the day-to-day use of the drug, adverse events, other related diseases, emotional and practical issues regarding compliance and/or discontinuation, etc. Our advanced CRM and AI systems allow our team to capture all those data points and insights, combine them with broader data about each patient (e.g., claims, electronic health records, etc.) and provide unique and actionable insights to the brand teams as they plan their commercial deployment and execution.

Of course, the privacy of patients is paramount. All data is anonymized, and only summary statistics are available. Complying with legal and - equally importantly - with ethical restrictions around the use of patient data is core to our DNA and the way we operate.
